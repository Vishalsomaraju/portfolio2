# Animation Guidelines

## Philosophy
Animation is functional, not decorative.

## Motion Levels
**Micro (hover)**
- Duration: 0.2–0.4s
- Use Framer Motion
- Smooth spring

**Medium (section)**
- Duration: 0.6–0.8s
- GSAP ScrollTrigger

**Macro (hero)**
- Duration: 1–1.4s
- Easing: power4.out

## Rules
- No random animations
- No excessive bounce
- No overlapping conflicting motion systems

## Scroll Behavior
- Smooth via Lenis
- Scroll should feel like a continuous experience

## Anti-patterns
- Animation for no reason
- Too many elements animating at once
- Janky transitions

Motion must feel intentional and premium.
